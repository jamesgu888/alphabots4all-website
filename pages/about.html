About Alphabots4All

Empowering the next generation through STEM education and robotics

Who We Are

Alphabots4All is more than just a robotics team‚Äîwe're a passionate community of innovators and educators based in Fremont, California. Our journey spans five transformative years in FIRST Tech Challenge, evolving from curious minds into a thriving community of 15 dedicated students spanning grades 6-12, with a dual mission of excelling in competitive robotics while inspiring the next generation of STEM leaders.

Our Footprint

Alphabots is an exemplary FIRST ambassador. Between 2024 and 2025, we have achieved many significant contributions to our local Bay Area neighborhood by giving back to the FIRST community, inspiring thousands of people through STEM and FIRST, and fostering professional connections. Over the season, we have demonstrated our long-term commitment to FIRST.
