FTC Regional Championship Results

Competition FTC Achievement

Our team competed in the FIRST Tech Challenge Regional Championship, demonstrating our innovative robot design and programming skills. We're proud to announce our advancement to the state level competition.

Competition Highlights:

  • Advanced to state championship
  • Scored highest in autonomous period
  • Team collaboration award recipient
  • Innovative robot design recognition

The competition featured teams from across the region, each presenting unique solutions to this year's challenge. Our robot's autonomous capabilities and precise mechanical design set us apart from the competition.

We're now preparing for the state championship in January, where we'll compete against the best teams from across California. This achievement represents countless hours of dedication from our team members and mentors.

Technical Achievements:

Our robot demonstrated exceptional performance in multiple areas:

  • Autonomous Navigation: Perfect navigation accuracy with advanced sensor fusion
  • Mechanical Design: Innovative intake mechanism with 95% efficiency
  • Programming: Robust error handling and adaptive algorithms
  • Team Collaboration: Seamless coordination during tele-operated periods

This success wouldn't have been possible without the dedication of our team members, the guidance of our mentors, and the support of our sponsors. We're excited to represent our region at the state level!
